    Basic Classess

  • margin and padding .m and .p
  • top, bottom, left, right .t .b .l .r
  • Height and width.h and .w
  • background and color.b-{hex color having second letter 0 like f0f0f0}-x and .c-{hex color having second letter 0 like f0f0f0}-x
