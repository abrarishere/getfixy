Configuration

We can configure classes for FiXY, such as your colors height and the default value from following classes by <style> tag in internal css in <head> tag.

As, When i have to change .h10 that is equal to 10px and we want to equal 100 than just in <head> tag we will say .h10{height:100px;} and this will be done.
